About Me

I'm passionate about creativity and have been since I was little. Growing up, I spent countless hours in the kitchen, baking delicious treats, and immersed in various craft projects with my mom. About five years ago, I discovered a love for sewing, and it quickly became a significant part of my creative journey. I especially enjoy making clothes that are made to my size!


Besides sewing, I find joy in unleashing my creativity with my Cricut machine, crafting intricate cards and other paper projects. Baking desserts is another one of my favorite hobbies - there's nothing like the satisfaction of creating something sweet from scratch. I also enjoy making beaded jewelry and expressing myself through drawing.


When I'm not engrossed in the world of crafts, you can find me pursuing an active lifestyle. I love working out and spending time outdoors. Hanging out with friends brings me immense joy, and I cherish those moments of shared laughter and connection. To unwind, I often dive into thrilling audiobooks, as mysteries and suspense are currently my favorites. Life is an adventure, and I embrace each day with a blend of creativity, fitness, socializing, and a good audiobook to accompany me on the journey.
